In this talk, I shared our latest progress in leveraging machine learning and AI technologies to revolutionize the design and screening of battery materials. 

**Key content of the presentation include:**
* **Integrating AI with Battery Science**: Discussed how machine learning models can bridge the gap between microscopic electronic structures and macroscopic battery performance.
* **Interpretable ML**: Shared insights from my research on using interpretable data analysis to develop advanced hard carbon materials for sodium-ion batteries.
* **Future Trends**: Explored the potential of Large Language Models (LLMs) in accelerating high-throughput catalyst screening for Lithium-Sulfur systems.
